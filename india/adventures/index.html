Sarpass Trek

13,700 ft
Peak Altitude
58 km
Round Trip Distance
5 Days
Trek Duration

Sarpass Trek in Himachal Pradesh remains one of my most rewarding mountain experiences. At 13,700 feet, Sarpass Pass offers panoramic views of snow-capped peaks and alpine meadows that extend as far as the eye can see. The 58-kilometer round journey tested both physical endurance and mental resilience, but every step was worth it.

What struck me most wasn't just the altitude or the distance, but the quiet moments at dawn when the world felt still. Standing amidst clouds that floated below me, I realized that many of life's profound insights come when we push beyond our comfort zones. The trek taught me the importance of persistence, preparation, and finding peace in the unknown.
