About

I'm from Pune, India — a city that shaped my curiosity and drive to build. My engineering journey began at SPIT (Pune Institute of Computer Technology) where I studied Electronics & Telecommunication. During my undergrad, I developed a passion for bridging theory and practice, leading me into full-stack development, AI/ML, and computer vision research.

My first professional role was at Dragonfly Financial Technologies, where I honed my backend development skills, working on payment systems and optimizing database queries. This evolved into my current role at Fidelity Information Services (FIS), where I contribute to the Payments team, handling complex international transactions and building robust financial software that serves millions globally. I'm driven by the challenge of solving real-world problems at scale.

Beyond work, I'm deeply invested in research—exploring applications of deep learning in healthcare (diabetic retinopathy classification) and speech recognition (lipreading for Indian English). I believe technology should serve humanity, and I'm committed to continuous learning and growth.
