About

Ireland represents a turning point in my journey. Starting my MSc at Trinity College Dublin in September 2025, I'm pursuing specialized knowledge in Computer Science with a focus on AI/ML, distributed systems, and emerging technologies. Coming from the fast-paced world of fintech, I'm now stepping back to engage deeply with research and theoretical foundations.

Trinity represents more than just a degree. It's an opportunity to collaborate with world-class researchers, contribute to cutting-edge projects, and explore questions that fascinate me. Ireland itself captivates me—the blend of ancient heritage with modern innovation, the thoughtful pace of life that contrasts with corporate hustle, and a community that values both intellectual rigor and human connection.

This chapter is about depth, discovery, and deliberate growth. I'm excited to push the boundaries of what I can contribute to the field while experiencing a different culture and perspective. By 2027, I aim to emerge not just as a better engineer, but as someone who can think critically, conduct original research, and drive meaningful innovation.
