Trinity College Dublin — MSc Computer Science (2025–2027)

My MSc at Trinity College Dublin represents a deliberate investment in depth and research. The program offers flexibility to tailor my studies around AI/ML, distributed systems, and computer vision — areas where I want to make meaningful contributions.

Core Focus Areas:

  • Advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning architectures
  • Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Research methodologies and academic writing

Beyond coursework, I'm actively pursuing research opportunities that align with my interests in medical AI and speech processing. Trinity's collaborative environment and access to world-class faculty make it an ideal place to transition from industry-focused development to research-driven innovation.
