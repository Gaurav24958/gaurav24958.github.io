Vision

By 2027, I aspire to have published research, contributed meaningfully to open-source projects, and positioned myself as someone who can bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications. I want to work on problems that matter—whether it's improving healthcare through computer vision, optimizing financial systems, or advancing AI safety and ethics.

Most importantly, I want to continue growing as both an engineer and a person. The journey doesn't end in 2027; it's just the next chapter. I'm excited about what comes next.
